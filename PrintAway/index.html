Materials and Parts

Built for Function, Fit, and Repeatability

We produce custom parts for prototypes, jigs, fixtures, and low-volume production using materials selected for your use case.

Typical dimensional tolerance is +/-0.20 mm (and about +/-0.2% on larger parts). Tight-fit features may require post-processing depending on geometry and material.

  • Functional prototypes and concept models
  • End-use brackets, covers, and adapters
  • Custom fixtures and tooling aids
  • Small-batch production components

Available Materials

PLA, PETG, ABS, ASA, TPU, PA-CF (Nylon Carbon Fiber), and support-ready engineering blends based on application.

Printer Fleet

Bambu Lab X1 Carbon, Bambu Lab P1P, and Bambu Lab A1 mini for fast prototyping and production flexibility.

Precision Notes

Critical dimensions can be tuned with print orientation, compensation, and test coupons before full production.

Maximum Build Sizes

X1 Carbon and P1P: 256 x 256 x 256 mm. A1 mini: 180 x 180 x 180 mm. Larger assemblies can be split and joined.

Multi-Color Capability

4-color prints available for logos, labeling, visual differentiation, and client-facing prototypes.

What We Can Make

Prototypes, housings, enclosures, brackets, fixtures, jigs, product mockups, and low-volume production parts.
