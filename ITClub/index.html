MHS IT Club

This is a temporary website for the MHS IT Club.

Here at IT Club, we strive to teach students about programming and computers in any way we can.

Since MHS does not offer the kind of IT classes we are interested in, we decided to start this club ourselves.

Our goal is to teach programming in more depth, whether that’s C, Python, or even Rust. At the end of the day, we’re here to learn and have fun.

Soon we will have a schedule posted so everyone knows what’s happening over the next 30 days.
